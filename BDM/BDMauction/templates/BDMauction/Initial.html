Instructions

Auction and Your Payment:

You are invited to participate in an auction to elicit your willingness-to-pay. You will be asked to submit your bid, it should be your maximum willingness-to-pay for the particular item.

After submitting your bid, the computer will also generate a random selling price (the price is drawn between $0 and the maximum price of the item). If the random selling price is greater than your bid price, you will not purchase the item. Otherwise, if the random selling price is less than or equal to your bid price, you will purchase the item.
