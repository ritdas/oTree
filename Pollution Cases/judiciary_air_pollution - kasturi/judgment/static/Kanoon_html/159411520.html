                             ORDER

D.H.WAGHELA, C.J. (Oral) :

1. The petition was not pressed, with a view to making appropriate application for valid consent under the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974 and the Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981, for permitting re-starting the unit of the petitioner.

2. Permission being granted, the petition is dismissed as not pressed.
