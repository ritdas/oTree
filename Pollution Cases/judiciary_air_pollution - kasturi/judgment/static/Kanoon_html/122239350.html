:ORDER

(Order of the Court was made by S.MANIKUMAR, J.) The petitioner has sought for a Writ of Mandamus, directing the respondents 1 to 5 to take necessary action against the 6th Respondent's illegal Plastic Unit in the name of Kamatchi Amman Plastics and to close the Unit permanently.

2.The District Environmental Engineer, Tamil Nadu Pollution Control Board, Thanjavur has filed a counter dated 29.02.2016, wherein, at paragraph Nos.6 and 7, he has stated as follows:

?6. It is respectfully submitted that, the unit was inspected on 18/02/2016, during inspection the unit is in operation without obtaining the building plan approval from Local Planning authority and without the consent of the Board. Hence, inspection report with the recommendation of issue of direction for closure and disconnection of power supply to the unit was sent to the Board vide this office IR No.:F.TNJNA/OS/DEE/TNJ/2016/ Dated:18/02/2016.

7. It is respectfully submitted, that, Board has issued direction order for stoppage of Electricity and closure of the industry under section 31A of AIR (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981 as amended vide Board's Proc.No.T8/F.MO238/TNPCB/OS/TNJ/EB/2016 Dated:25/02/2016 and Proc.No.T8/F.MO238/TNPCB/OS/TNJ/A/2016 Dated:25/02/2016. Power supply was disconnected to the unit by Assistant Engineer/(Operation & Maintenane), Town West/TANGEDCO, Kumbakonam, Kumbakonam Taluk, Thanjavur District on 29.02.2016.?

3.In the light of the statement, there is no need to issue any mandamus. Recording the above statement, the Writ Petition is closed. No costs. Consequently, connected Miscellaneous Petitions are closed.
