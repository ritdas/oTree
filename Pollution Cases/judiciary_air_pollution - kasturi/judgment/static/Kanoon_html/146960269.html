Noticeably, out of 60 establishments (hotels), 44 establishments are neither having permissions from the Tourism Department nor do they have any consent to operate in terms of Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974 and Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981. As such, we direct all such 44 establishments be shut down forthwith. The Deputy Commissioner, Kullu, to ensure that the water and electricity supply connections to these establishments are also disconnected forthwith. If necessary, Superintendent of Police, Kullu, shall depute police force for implementing such directions.

Noticeably, some of these establishments have also encroached upon forest land and perhaps raised construction thereupon.

We direct the Deputy Commissioner, Kullu, to act as a Nodal Officer and coordinate with all the relevant Departments of District Kullu for ensuring that all encroachments, so identified are removed and unauthorized structures demolished. Also no establishments at Village Kasol be allowed to operate, save and except, in accordance with law.
