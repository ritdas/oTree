Bench: Pankaj Kumar Jaiswal, Karunesh Singh Pawar



HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT ALLAHABAD, LUCKNOW BENCH
 
 


 

 
Court No. - 1
 

 
Case :- MISC. BENCH No. - 4531 of 2020
 

 
Petitioner :- Shamshul Haq
 
Respondent :- State Of U.P.Through Secy.Environment And Ors.
 
Counsel for Petitioner :- Raj Kumar Verma,Pradeep Kumar Kajaujiya
 
Counsel for Respondent :- C.S.C.,A.K.Verma
 

 
Hon'ble Pankaj Kumar Jaiswal,J.

Hon'ble Karunesh Singh Pawar,J.

Heard Sri Raj Kumar Verma, learned Counsel for the petitioner, Sri Shailendra Singh Chauhan, learned Additional Chief Standing Counsel for respondent Nos.1, 2 and 5 and Sri A. K. Verma, learned Counsel for U. P. Pollution Control Board/respondent Nos.3 and 4.

Order impugned dated 30.9.2019 passed by the Chief Environment Officer, Lucknow is appealable under Section 33-B (c) of the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974 before the National Green Tribunal.

In view of above, we grant liberty to the petitioner to challenge the aforesaid order by availing the aforesaid remedy as provided in the Statute.

With the aforesaid liberty, the writ petition is dismissed as withdrawn.
