2. Questions of Law:

A. Whether the Respondent Commission failed to appreciate that the Petition filed by Ramnad Renewable Energy Ltd. was regulatory and not adjudicatory in nature?

B. Whether the Respondent Commission acted contrary to:- 

      (a)     the settled position of law that:-

              (i)    "regulatory"   and   "adjudicatory"   functions     of    an
