SATISH KUMAR MITTAL, J. ( Oral ) Learned counsel prays that the petitioner may be permitted to withdraw this petition with liberty to avail the remedy of appeal provided under Section 31 of the Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981.
