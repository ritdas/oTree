                         ORDER

None appeared for the petitioner on the last date. Even today, none appears for the petitioner. The challenge in this writ petition under Article 226 of the Constitution of India is to a direction issued under Section 31A of the Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981. The statutory remedy of an appeal under Section 31 of the said Act is available to challenge the said order. Hence we decline to entertain this petition. Accordingly, the petition is disposed of. However, statutory remedy is kept open.
