Allahabad High Court
M/S Laghu Udyog Pakai Bhatti ... vs The Commissioner Agra Region ... on 28 February, 2020
Bench: Govind Mathur, Chief Justice, Samit Gopal



HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT ALLAHABAD
 
 

?Chief Justice's Court
 

 
Case :- WRIT - C No. - 7313 of 2020
 

 
Petitioner :- M/S Laghu Udyog Pakai Bhatti Chamber Sahkari Samiti Through Its Proprietor
 
Respondent :- The Commissioner Agra Region Through Its Chairman And 2 Others
 
Counsel for Petitioner :- Radhey Shyam Dwivedi
 
Counsel for Respondent :- C.S.C.,Hari Nath Tripathi, J.N. Maurya
 

 
Hon'ble Govind Mathur,Chief Justice
 
Hon'ble Samit Gopal,J.

This petition for writ is preferred to challenge an order dated 17.01.2020 passed on behalf of Uttar Pradesh Pollution Control Board.

Such an order is appealable under Section 31 of the Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981 (hereinafter referred to as "Act, 1981").

In the case in hand, we do not find any just reason to deviate from the general principle to approach writ Court only after exhausting all alternative remedies.

In view of it, this petition for writ is dismissed. The petitioner is at liberty to avail the remedy given under the Act, 1981.

Order Date :- 28.2.2020 Shubham .

(Samit Gopal, J.) (Govind Mathur, C.J.)
