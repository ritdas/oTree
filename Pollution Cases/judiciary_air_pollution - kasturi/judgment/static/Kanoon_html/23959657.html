MAHABIR SINGH SINDHU, J. (ORAL)

Present petition has been filed under Section 482 Cr.P.C. praying for quashing of Complaint No.32 of 2015 dated 08.05.2015 (Annexure P-1), filed by the respondent/complainant under Sections 43, 44 read with Section 47 of the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974 as well as summoning order dated 17.09.2014 (Annexure P-2), passed by the learned Chief judicial Magistrate, Faridkot.

After arguing for sometime, learned Counsel for the petitioners wishes to withdraw the present petition with liberty to raise all his pleas before the learned trial Court at the appropriate stage.

In view of above, the present petition is dismissed as withdrawn with the aforesaid liberty. 

May 31, 2018                              ( MAHABIR SINGH SINDHU )
