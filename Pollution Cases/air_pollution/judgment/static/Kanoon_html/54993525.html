2/ 17.11.2014

Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned counsel for the State.

The petitioner is apprehending his arrest in connection with C.P. Case No. 30 of 2013, for the alleged offences under Sections 37, 38 and 39 of the Air (Prevention & Control of Pollution) Act, 1981.

From the complaint petition filed by the Regional Officer, Jharkhand State Pollution Control Board, it is apparent that the factory of the petitioner was found in production in spite of the fact that the closure notice was issued to the petitioner's factory by the Pollution Control Board.

In the facts of this case, I am not inclined to grant anticipatory bail to petitioner, Rajesh Kumar Karmali @ Rajesh Karmali. Accordingly, his prayer for anticipatory bail is rejected.

( H. C. Mishra, J.) R.Kr.
