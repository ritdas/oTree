Punjab-Haryana High Court
Krishan Lal And Another vs State Of Haryana And Others on 11 October, 2013
           IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA
                         AT CHANDIGARH

                                            CWP No. 22781 of 2013
                                            Date of Decision : 11.10.2013

Krishan Lal and another                                   ....... Petitioners


                                   Versus


State of Haryana and others                               ..... Respondents

CORAM: HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE SANJAY KISHAN KAUL, CHIEF JUSTICE HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE AUGUSTINE GEORGE MASIH Present:- Mr. Keshav Gupta, Advocate, for the petitioners. SANJAY KISHAN KAUL, CHIEF JUSTICE (ORAL) The dispute relates to the order dated 24.7.2013 issued under Section 33-A of the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974 and Section 31-A of the Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981, directing closure.

The aforesaid order is an appealable order before the National Green Tribunal and taking into consideration that a specialized Tribunal has been formed for this purpose, it is appropriate that the grievance is agitated before the Tribunal.

We thus, dismiss the petition with liberty to the petitioner to approach the Tribunal for examining the case on merits.
