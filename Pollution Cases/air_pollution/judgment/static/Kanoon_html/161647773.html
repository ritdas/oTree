Jharkhand High Court
Ramesh Prasad vs State Of Jharkhand on 9 August, 2012
           IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI

                         B.A. No. 4453 of 2012

           Ramesh Prasad             .........Petitioner
                        Vs.
           The State of Jharkhand    ........Opposite party

CORAM:     HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE PRASHANT KUMAR

           For the Petitioner:        Mr. Vijay Shankar Prasad
           For the State:             Mr. S.P. Jha , APP

2/09.08.2012

: Bail application filed by petitioner Ramesh Prasad is moved by Sri. Vijay Shankar Prasad and opposed by Sri. S.P. Jha , learned Additional P.P.

This is a case under sections 379/34 of the IPC, 14 of the Pollution Act and 48, 53 and 54 of Jharkhand Minor Minerals Concession Rule 2004. It appears that the co-accused have been granted bail by the court below and bail prayer of the petitioner rejected because of his antecedent. However, it is stated at paragraph no. 13 of the bail application that petitioner involved in only one case under section 307 of the IPC which resulted in his acquittal.

Considering the aforesaid facts and circumstance, I allow this application and direct the court below to enlarge the petitioner, above named, on bail on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/(Ten Thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned C.J.M., Latehar in connection with Latehar P.S. Case No. 35 of 2012 ( G.R. No. 169 of 2012). ( Prashant Kumar,J.) Sharda/-
