2/19.11.2014: The present petition has been filed under Section 482 Cr.P.C. for quashing the entire criminal proceeding including the First Information Report being Karra P.S. Case No. 72/2013 (G.R. No. 495/2013) for the offence punishable under Sections 379 and 411 IPC, Section 4(i) of the Mines and Minerals (Regulation and Development) Act, 1957 and Section 21 of the Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981, pending in the court of learned Judicial Magistrate, 1st Class, Khunti.

Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner as well as the learned A.P.P. appearing on behalf of the State and perusal the materials placed on record.

Learned counsel for the petitioner submitted that the Sub-Inspector of Police instituted the present FIR against the petitioner, who is not having the authority under law to institute such FIR for the alleged offence under Sections 379 and 411 IPC, Section 4(i) of the Mines and Minerals (Regulation and Development) Act, 1957 and Section 21 of the Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981. It is further submitted that in view of Section 22 of the Mines and Minerals (Regulation and Development) Act, 1957, which specifically provides that no court shall take cognizance of any offence punishable under this Act or any rules made thereunder except upon complaint in writing made by a person authorised in this behalf by the Central Government or the State Government. In support of his submission, learned counsel for the petitioner also referred to and relied upon the judgment passed in 2009(2)JCR 509(Jhr.) and 2010(3) JCR 301(Jhr.).

Per contra, the learned APP appearing for the State prays some time to place the case law against the submission made by the learned counsel for the petitioner pertaining to question of law.

Put up this case tomorrow i.e. on 20.11.2014.
