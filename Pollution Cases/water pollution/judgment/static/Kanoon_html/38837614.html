                            ORDER

The impugned corrigendum modifies the direction issued earlier under Section 33(A) of the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974 (for short 'the said Act'). Therefore, the impugned corrigendum dated 12th July 2019 is also issued in exercise of power under Section 33(A) of the said Act.

2. The remedy of preferring a substantive appeal under sub-section (c) of Section 33-B of the said Act is available to the petitioner. The contentions which are raised in the petition can be always agitated in the appeal. On the ground of availability of an efficacious remedy, we decline to entertain this petition and the same is accordingly disposed of. The remedy of the petitioner is kept open.
