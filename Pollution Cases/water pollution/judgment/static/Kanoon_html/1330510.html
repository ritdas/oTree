ORAL
ORDER

(Per : HONOURABLE THE CHIEF JUSTICE MR. S.J. MUKHOPADHAYA) The status report submitted by the Gujarat Pollution Control Board is incomplete. No specific inquiry was made in the report submitted under Air or Water Acts with regard to Ashapura Mine Chem Ltd. nor any such inquiry made under Water Act with respect to Mahashakti Coke and Jindal Saw Ltd.

One more opportunity is given to the Gujarat Pollution Control Board to make such inquiry under the Air and Water Acts with regard to all the industries and submit a report, failing which, Court may pass appropriate orders.

Post the mater on 27th January, 2010.
