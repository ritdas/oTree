3 20-06-2012 Learned counsel for the petitioner is permitted to make necessary correction in the prayer portion of the petition.

Heard learned counsels for the petitioner and the State. The petitioner is apprehending arrest in a case registered for the offences punishable under Section 379 of the Indian Penal Code, Section 4, 40 and 26 of the B.M.M.C. Act, 21A of the Air Act and 31 of the Water Act.

The accusation is of running the brick kiln without valid authorization and thereby causing loss to the government to the tune of Rs. 52,000/-.

It is submitted by learned counsel for the petitioner that without verification of the fact that the brick kiln was running, the loss has been calculated.

Without entering into merit of the case, the petitioner is ready to deposit Rs. 25,000/- before the learned court below which will be invested in some fixed deposit scheme which will be Patna High Court Cr.Misc. No.18821 of 2012 (3) dt.20-06-2012 2/2 subject to the result of the case.

On deposit of the aforesaid amount, let the above named petitioner be released on anticipatory bail, in the event of arrest or surrender before the learned Court below within a period of 12 weeks from today, on furnishing bail bond of Rs. 10,000/- (ten thousand) with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of the learned Chief Judicial Magistrate, Jehanabad in connection with Makhdumpur P.S. Case No. 352 of 2010, subject to the conditions as laid down under Section 438(2) Cr.P.C.

(Dinesh Kumar Singh, J) Amrendra/-
