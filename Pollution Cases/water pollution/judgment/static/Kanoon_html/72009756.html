05/Dated: 13/06/2018
         The petitioners            are apprehending their arrest in

connection with the case registered under Sections 25, 26, 21 of Water Act, Section 21 of Air Act, Sections 6(1) of the Factory Act and 33(b) (c) of the Indian Forest Act.

Learned counsel for the petitioner has submitted that the petitioners have been falsely implicated in order to harass and humiliate. It is further submitted that the petitioners are neither owner nor they have any connection with the alleged crushed plants.

Learned APP has opposed the prayer for bail. Be that as it may, I am inclined to admit the above named petitioners on anticipatory bail. The petitioners are directed to surrender in the court below within four weeks and in the event of their arrest or surrender, the court below shall release them on bail on their furnishing bail bond of Rs.15,000/- (Fifteen Thousand) each with two sureties of the like amount each to the satisfaction of learned Judicial Magistrate, 1st Class, Garhwa, in connection with Kharaundhi P.S. Case No.68 of 2017, corresponding to G.R.No.1781/17, subject to the condition as laid down under Section 438(2) of the Cr.P.C., and subject to further conditions is that on the date of surrender petitioner nos. 1, 2 and 3 shall deposit Rs.10,000/-, 4,000/- and 12,000/- respectively before the court below, which shall be subject to result of the case and will not prejudice the case of the petitioners.

(Anant Bijay Singh, J.) fahim/
