                            O R D E R

The petitioners herein seek pre-arrest bail under Section 438 of the Code of Criminal Procedure on the apprehension of arrest in connection with Crime No.465/2016 of the Perinthalmanna Police Station, registered under Sections 269 IPC, 120(e) of the Kerala Police Act, and under Section 24(1) read with Section 43 of the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974 (for short, 'the Act'). The offence alleged under Section 24(1) of the Act is non-bailable.

2. The prosecution allegation is that on 03.06.2016 the petitioners dumped toilet waste at the side of the public road at Eravimangalam. Depositing any poisonous, or noxious, or polluting matter in any stream, or well, or on land is made punishable under the said section by way of an amendment brought in 1988. The exact nature of the allegations, as to whether the allegations will definitely come under this section, will have to be probed into by the learned Magistrate having jurisdiction. In the nature of the allegations, I feel it B.A.Nos.5347 of 2016 2 appropriate to direct the petitioners to surrender before the investigating officer for necessary interrogation. Their interrogation for the collection of materials concerning the nature of the substances, the different instances where such materials were deposited by the petitioners, the role and complicity of each of the accused, etc., is felt necessary. After such interrogation, appropriate orders on the request for bail can be passed by the learned Magistrate. If detention of the petitioners in custody is not required in the particular facts and circumstances, the petitioners can be granted bail on appropriate conditions.

In the result, this petition is disposed of as follows:

a) The petitioners shall surrender before the investigating officer, between 9 am and 10 am on any day within seven days from this date for necessary interrogation.

b) After interrogation, the investigating officer will produce the petitioners before the learned Magistrate having jurisdiction, before 4 pm on the same day.

c) In case application for regular bail is filed, the same shall be judiciously considered and decided by B.A.Nos.5347 of 2016 3 the learned Magistrate on the same day.
