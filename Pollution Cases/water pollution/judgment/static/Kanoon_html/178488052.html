      Shri Yogesh Kumar Gupta, learned counsel for the
applicant.
      Shri Pankaj Wadhwani, learned Public Prosecutor for

      Heard. Case diary perused.
      This is first bail application under Section 439 of Cr.P.C.
for grant of bail in connection with Crime No.1114/2018
registered at Police Station - Banganga, District - Indore for
commission of offence punishable under Sections 268, 269,
277, 278, 284, 308, and 34 of IPC and sections 25, 26 & 44
of Water Pollution & Control Act, 1974. The applicant is in
custody since 3/11/2018.
      As per the prosecution case, on 03/10/2018 the police
Banganga got an information from the informer that injured
Naresh was brought to the hospital by one Naveen as he
suffered certain reactions because of some chemical was
flowing in the Naala. On the basis of the said information,
police reached at the place of incidence and during
investigation, they found that the applicant was cleaning the
offending vehicle in the Naala, due to which the foam came
out and the eyes of the people was burning. During
investigation,   accused has stated that he took the said
chemical from Geeta Shree Garden and the owner and of the
said garden is Manohar Porwal and the owner of the tanker is
Yogendra Chandrawat.       It is also alleged that due to said
chemical Jitendra, Liyakat and Naresh were injured. On the

                 Learned counsel for the applicant submits that       the

           opposes the application and submits that as per oral and
           material evidence which has come in the case diary, there is
           ample evidence against the present applicant and, therefore,
           no case is made out for enlarging the applicant on bail.
                 Heard the arguments of the learned counsel for the
           parties and perused the case diary.
                 After taking into consideration all the facts and
           circumstances of the case, I do not find it fit to release the
           applicant on bail.
                 M.Cr.C. No.1164/2019 filed by the applicant has no
           merit and is accordingly dismissed.
