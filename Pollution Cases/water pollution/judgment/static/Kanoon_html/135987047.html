                             O R D E R

Petition filed under Section 438 Cr.P.C.

2. The petitioner is the 2nd accused in Crime No.2594/2016 of Chalakudy Police Station registered for the offences under Section 269 IPC, 120(e) of the Kerala Police Act and Section 24(1) read with Section 43 of the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act. The prosecution case is that he along with the co-accused dumped night soil into the river, the water of which is used by the people of the locality for their domestic purposes.

3. Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner and the learned Public Prosecutor.

4. It is submitted by the learned Public Prosecutor that final report has already been filed. It appears that the petitioner's apprehension that they will be arrested by the police is not reasonable. They may appear before the Magistrate and apply for bail.

B.A.No.1383 of 2017 2

In the result, this application is dismissed with the above observations.
