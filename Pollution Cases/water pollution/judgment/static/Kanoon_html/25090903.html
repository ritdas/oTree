                              The   petitioner     is   aggrieved   because   his

                  application for consent to establish and operate a brick

                  field has not yet been disposed of by the West Bengal

                  Pollution Control Board.

                              It appears from the instructions received by

                  Mr. Roy Chowdhury, learned senior Government advocate

                  for the State from the Additional District Magistrate &

                  District Land & Land Reforms Officer, Burdwan that

                               "...It is also to state that the instant brick field
                    in the name and style JYOTISH BRICK FIELD is
                    operating as unauthorized brick field having no valid
                    Consent to Establish (CTE) and/or Consent to Operate
                    (CTO) from the Pollution Control Board, West Bengal and
                    as such was made a respondent being no.851 in O.A.

No.39/2014EZ before the Hon'ble National Green Tribunal. The Hon'ble National Green Tribunal has issued show cause notices to all such unauthorized brick fields including the instant brick field for operating their brick klins illegally. The Hon'ble Tribunal is also of the view that those brick fields had breached the statutory provisions of Air Act, 1981 and Water Act, 1974 and have contributed to environment pollution and soil degradation of the locality and as such are liable to pay penalty for violation of environmental norms."

2

Since O.A.39/2014EZ is still pending before the National Green Tribunal, Kolkata Bench, it shall be open to the petitioner to seek appropriate orders in such proceeding.

Reserving the liberty of the petitioner to approach the tribunal, this writ petition stands disposed of.

There shall be no order as to costs.

Urgent photostat certified copy of this order, if applied for, be furnished to the parties expeditiously.

(DIPANKAR DATTA,J.)
