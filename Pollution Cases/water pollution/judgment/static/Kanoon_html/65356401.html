                          ORDER

The petitioners herein are the accused Nos.1 to 3 in crime No.1055/2017 of Irinjalakuda Police Station for offences punishable under Sections 24(1) & 43 of Water Pollution Act, 1974 r/w Section 269 of IPC and 120(e) of K.P Act.

2. The prosecution alleged that on 10.07.2017, the petitioners had unloaded toilet waste in a thodu and thereby polluted it. They were apprehended on the same day and are in custody since then.

3. It appears that they are not involved in any other crime. The investigation seems to have substantially progressed. Hence, I do not feel that a further custody of the petitioners would be justified. Hence, I am inclined to grant bail to the petitioners herein on the following conditions:

B.A. No.5194 of 2017 2

(i) Petitioners shall execute a bond for a sum of Rs.40,000/- (Rupees forty thousand only) with two sureties for the like sum each to the satisfaction of the jurisdictional Court.
(ii) They shall appear before the investigating officer as and when called for.
(iii) They shall not threaten, coerce or intimidate the defacto complainant and the witnesses nor shall they interfere in the process of investigation.
(iv) They shall not get involved in any other offences.
Bail application is allowed as above.
