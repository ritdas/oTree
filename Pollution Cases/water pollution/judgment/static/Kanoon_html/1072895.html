                            J U D G M E N T

~~~~~~~~~~~ The petitioner was granted a building permit to erect a Mobile Telecommunication Tower. On completion of that Tower, the Panchayat took the stand that the User Certificate cannot be issued without the clearance of the Pollution Control Board. By interim order dated 15.2.2008, the structure is numbered, provisionally.

2. The Environmental Engineer is a party in this Writ Petition. The Panchayat was not unjustified in its stand because the Environmental Engineer had issued Ext.R1(a). But, there is no provision of law pointed out, requiring the clearance of the Pollution Control Board, except in cases, where the activities of the Tower would result in activities referable to the Air Act or Water Act. To that extend, it is for the Pollution Control Board authorities to insist on clearance, but not to scuttle the issuance of the permit. Therefore, this Writ Petition is allowed, quashing the impugned Ext.P2 and directing the Panchayat to grant User Certificate and permit the W.P(C) No.2928//2008 2 petitioner to operate without prejudice to the powers of the Pollution Control Board to take action, in accordance with law, if found necessary.

The Writ Petition is ordered, accordingly.
