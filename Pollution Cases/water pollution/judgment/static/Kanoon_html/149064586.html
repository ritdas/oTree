                           JUDGMENT

Ext.P6 is an appeal preferred by the petitioner under Section 28 of the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974 challenging Ext.P5, before the seventh respondent. The limited prayer made by the learned counsel for the petitioner, when this matter was taken up for admission, is for a direction to the seventh respondent to take a decision on Ext.P6 appeal.

2. Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner, the learned counsel for the sixth respondent, the learned Government Pleader as also the learned Standing Counsel for the second respondent.

3. Having regard to the facts and circumstances of the case, I deem it appropriate to direct the seventh respondent to take a decision on Ext.P6 appeal, within three W.P.(C) No.30129 of 2016 -2- weeks from the date of receipt of a copy of this judgment, after affording the petitioner as also the sixth respondent an opportunity of hearing. Ordered accordingly.

The writ petition is disposed of as above.
