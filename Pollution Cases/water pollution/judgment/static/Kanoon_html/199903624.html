           AJAY KUMAR MITTAL, J. (Oral)

The petitioner has approached this Court by way of present writ petition under Articles 226/227 of the Constitution of India for issuing a writ in the nature of mandamus directing the official respondent Nos. 1 to 4 to take necessary legal action against respondent No.5, who allegedly carrying on the industrial activity of manufacturing of Tractor Parts in the residential area without obtaining any No Objection Certificate or requisite permissions from the competent authorities including consent to operate under the provisions of the Air Act, 1981 and Water Act, 1974.

2. After arguing for some time, learned counsel for the petitioner states that he may be allowed to withdraw the present writ petition with liberty to file afresh one by incorporating better particulars.

3. Dismissed as withdrawn with liberty as prayed for.
