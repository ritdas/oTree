Calcutta High Court (Appellete Side)
7371/2013 on 21 June, 2013
Author: Nishita Mhatre

1 C. R. M. No. 7371 of 2013 Re: An application under section 438 of the Code of Criminal Procedure filed on 23rd May, 2013.

In Re: Goutam Dutta .. Petitioner.

Mr. Milon Mukherjee, Mr. Usof Ali Dewan .. For the Petitioner.

Mr. Prasun Kumar Dutta, Mr. Subrata Roy .. For the State.

The petitioner apprehending arrest in connection with Raghunathgunj Police Station Case No. 235 of rd 2013 dated 3 April, 2013 under section 188 the Indian Penal Code, section 44 of the Water Pollution Act, section 37(1) of the Air Act and Section 15 of the Environment Act, 1986 has come to this court for anticipatory bail.

We have considered the case diary and other material on record. We do not think that there is any need for the custodial interrogation of the petitioner in this case because the petitioner has been given consent to operate the brick-field and the permission has been renewed on payment of a penalty.

In these circumstances, in the event of arrest the petitioner shall be released on bail upon furnishing a bond of ` 10,000/- with two sureties, one of whom must be local surety, to the satisfaction of the court concerned subject to the conditions stipulated in section 438(2) of the Code of Criminal Procedure.

The application for anticipatory bail is disposed of.

(Nishita Mhatre, J.) (Asim Kumar Ray, J.)
