Order No.5 Dated 3rd July, 2014

1. The appellant is represented by learned counsel Mr. Ashok Kumar Yadav and Jharkhand State Pollution Control Board is represented by Mr. Prabhash Kumar and Mr. A.K. Pandey, Advocates.

2. The present appeal is filed setting aside the order and judgment dated 22.4.2013 passed in W.P.C No. 5703 of 2012, whereby the writ petition was disposed of by giving direction to the appellant to install the Effluent Treatment Plant within twelve weeks from the date of order with further direction that if the installation of the Effluent Treatment Plant and any other compliance of Air Act or Water Act are not found to have been complied by the appellant, it will be open for the respondent Board to proceed in accordance with law.

3. This Letters Patent Appeal is listed for hearing, but after some arguments, learned counsel appearing for the appellant upon instructions received from his client seeks permission to withdraw this appeal.

4. Learned counsel for the Pollution Control Board has submitted that dispite the specific direction given by the Court for installation of Effluent Treatment Plant within 12 weeks from the date of the order, the said plant has not been made functional. As against that the learned counsel for the appellant has submitted that due to technical reasons, the said plant could not be successfully installed.

5. Accordingly, this Letters Patent Appeal is dismissed as withdrawn.
