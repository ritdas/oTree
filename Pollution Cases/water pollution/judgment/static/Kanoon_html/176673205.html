                                                   ORDER

[Order of the Court was made by K.K.SASIDHARAN, J.] The learned counsel for the petitioner made an endorsement indicating that he is withdrawing the Writ Petition with liberty to approach the National Green Tribunal.

2. In view of the endorsement so made, the Writ Petition is dismissed as withdrawn with such liberty. However, we make it clear that the expression (liberty) should not be construed as an opinion on the merits of the matter. No costs. Consequently, WMP(MD)Nos.

22428 to 22430 and 22545 of 2018 are closed.
