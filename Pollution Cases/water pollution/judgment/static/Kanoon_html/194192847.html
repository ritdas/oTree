 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT JAIPUR BENCH JAIPUR

O R D E R
IN
S.B. CIVIL WRIT PETITION NO.7077/2016
(M/s. Laxmi Handicrafts Vs. Rajasthan State Pollution Control Board & Anr.)

Date of Order : 				                        31.05.2016

HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ALOK SHARMA

Mr. Prateek Kasliwal, for the petitioner.

BY THE COURT

Admittedly the impugned notice dated 10.05.2016, issued by the Member Secretary, Rajasthan State Pollution Control Board, Jaipur is amenable to appeal under the Water (Prevention of Control of Pollution) Act, 1974. The jurisdiction of this Court under Article 226 of the Constitution of India is discretionary. I am of the view that as the petitioner has an efficacious alternate remedy, it should be required to resort thereto.

It is directed that looking to the urgency in the matter, in the event of an appeal being filed by the petitioner against the impugned notice dated 10.05.2016, it be decided by a reasoned order within four weeks of its filing.

The writ petition stands disposed of accordingly.

Stay application needs no address in view of the petition itself having been disposed of.

(ALOK SHARMA), J MS/-64
