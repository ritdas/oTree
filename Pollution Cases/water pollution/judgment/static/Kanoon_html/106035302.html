                       ORDER

Grievance of the petitioner is with regard to the illegality in taking cognizance by the learned Magistrate, in respect of the offences under Section 25 of the Water Act r/w Section 44 of Water (Prevention & Control of Pollution) Act, 1974 and the summons issued to him. The said grievance can be the subject matter of consideration in a revision petition under Section 397 Cr.PC, as held in Urmila Devi Vs. Yaduvir Singh, (2013) 15 SCC 624.

-3-

Hence, this petition is disposed of by reserving liberty to the petitioner to avail the said statutory remedy.

All contentions raised in this petition are left open. If the remedy of revision is availed on or before 31.07.2015, the time taken in prosecution of this petition from 20.07.2012, shall stand excluded for the purpose of computation of limitation.

Interim order passed in this petition, shall operate till the date of filing of the criminal revision petition or 31.07.2015, whichever date is earlier.

Certified copies produced along with this petition, be returned to the learned advocate for the petitioner.
