                                 ORDER

The petitioners herein seek pre arrest bail under Section 438 of the Code of Criminal Procedure on the apprehension of arrest in connection with Crime No.458 of 2017 of the Nilambur Police Station, registered under Sections 270 IPC, 120(e) of the Kerala Police Act, Section 43 read with Section 24 of the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act (the Special Act), and Section 3(2)(a) of the PDPP Act. They are three accused in the crime.

2. The prosecution case is that these petitioners collected latrine waste from some hotels, transported it in tanker lorries, and deposited the waste at the river near the Vadapuram Bridge at Nilambur, in the night of 13.10.2017 and 14.10.2017. The police initiated action, and registered crime on a complaint made by the Assistant Engineer of the Kerala Water Authority, Wandoor. The main offence alleged against the petitioners is an offence against public health, and the allegation is serious also. Anyway, in the nature of the allegations, I feel it appropriate to direct the petitioners to surrender before the Investigating Officer for necessary interrogation. Without such interrogation, the investigation cannot B.A.No.7479 of 2017 2 be completed. Let the request for regular be considered by the court below having jurisdiction.

In the result, this application for bail is disposed of as follows;

a) The petitioners will surrender before the Investigating Officer between 9 a.m. and 10 a.m. on any day within seven days from this date for the purpose of interrogation as part of investigation.

b) After necessary interrogation, the Investigating Officer will produce the petitioners before the learned Magistrate having jurisdiction before 4 p.m. on the same day.

c) In case application for regular bail is filed by the petitioners, the same shall be judiciously considered and decided on the same day by the learned Magistrate.
