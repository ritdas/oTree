                          JUDGMENT

The petitioner is aggrieved by Ext. P12 notice issued by the second respondent and Ext. P15 notice issued by the third respondent even while Ext. P10 appeal filed against Ext. P8 is pending consideration. Ext. P10 appeal has been filed before the Tribunal against Ext. P8 decision refusing to issue licence to conduct a pig farm.

2. It is open to the petitioner to apply for appropriate interim reliefs in Ext. P10 appeal even against Exts. P12 and P15 notices. Any motion made in that regard within two weeks from today shall be dealt with by the Tribunal by four weeks therefrom.

3. The petitioner is also permitted to independently challenge Ext. P15 notice before the appellate authority. An appeal lies under the Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981.

The writ petition is disposed of.

V. CHITAMBARESH JUDGE DCS
