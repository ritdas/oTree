                           ORDER

Sri D.Nagaraj, learned Additional Government Advocate appears for the respondents.

2. Heard.

3. Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that since the Government of Karnataka has not appointed a Chairman to the Karnataka State Appellate Authority, Bengaluru under the Water and Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Acts, 1974 and 1981 respectively, he may be granted liberty to approach the National Green Tribunal ('NGT'). He further submits that similar petitions filed under the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974 are pending before the NGT. -3-

4. In view of the submissions made by learned counsel for the petitioner, these petitions stand disposed of reserving liberty to the petitioner to approach the NGT.
