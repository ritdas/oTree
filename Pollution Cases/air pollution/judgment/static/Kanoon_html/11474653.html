                             ORDER

The respondent/complainant lodged a private complaint against the petitioner/accused for the offences under Sections 21 and 31(A) punishable under Section 37 of the Air (Prevention & Control of Pollution) Act, 1981 ('Air Act', for short) and Sections 24 and 25 punishable under Sections 43 and 44 of the Water (Prevention & Control of Pollution) Act, 1974 ('Water Act', for short). Questioning the proceedings, the present petition is filed.

2. Heard learned counsels.

3. The complainant has narrated the manner in which the offence has been committed by the petitioner. That the provisions of Air Act and Water Act have been blatantly violated by the petitioner. Substantial material have been placed by the complainant in order to show that the petitioner is guilty of the offence alleged against him. Based on available material, I do not find any good ground to quash the proceedings. :3:

Consequently, the petition is rejected.
