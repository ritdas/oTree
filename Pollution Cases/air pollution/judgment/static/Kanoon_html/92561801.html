                            ORDER

K.SREEDHAR RAO, Ag.C.J. (Oral) :

Respondent no.3 is running a Sugar Factory. The petitioners have submitted that respondent no.3 has committed violations of the provisions of the Water and Air Pollution Acts and seek the following prayers in the Writ Petition.

" a) Issue a writ in the nature of mandamus or any other writ, direction or order, directing the respondent no.3 to stop the crushing operation forthwith till the respondent no.4 passes the consent order under the Provisions of Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981 and Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974.
b) Issue a writ in the nature of mandamus or any other writ, direction or order, directing the respondent no.4 to initiate criminal prosecution against the respondent no.3 for violating the provisions of Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981 and Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974 and
c) issue a writ in the nature of mandamus or any other writ, direction or order directing the respondent nos.1 and 2 to take steps to determine the crushing capacity of the 6 respondent no.3 and further to de-reserve the cane command area of the said respondent no.3."

2. Respondent no.3 submits that vide Annexure-R.10 dated 22.12.2012 in the proceedings of the Pollution Control Board, it is stated that 'the consent for the period 01.07.2012 to 30.06.2013 will be issued after receipt of time bound action plan on the above points from the industry'. The counsel submits that in response to the said communication the action plan has been submitted and there are no violations committed by respondent no.3.

3. Counsel for respondent no.4 submits that pursuant to Annexure-R.10 inspection has been made and certain violations under the Water and Air Pollution Acts have been noticed and that a Report is communicated to respondent no.3 for removal of the violations.

4. Counsel for respondent no.3 submits that so far no communication in that regard is received from respondent no.4.

5. Counsel for the petitioners under a memo has produced the Inspection Report of the Pollution Control Board where the 7 violations of Water and Air Pollution Acts are noted and recommendations are made for initiating criminal action also.

6. In view of the submissions made and the material on record, respondent no.4 is directed to take proper action in accordance with law after considering the response of respondent no.3 to the objections noted in the Inspection Report. Accordingly, the writ petition is disposed of.
