                              J U D G M E N T

Mohan M. Shantanagoudar, CJ In view of the availability of alternative and efficacious remedy of appeal under Section 31 of the Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981 and Section 28 of the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974, we decline to entertain the writ petition. The writ petition fails and the same stands dismissed, however, with liberty to the petitioners to approach the appellate authority within one month from this date. All the issues are left open.
