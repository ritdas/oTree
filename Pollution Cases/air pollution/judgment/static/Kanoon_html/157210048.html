HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE AT ALLAHABAD, LUCKNOW BENCH
 
 


 

 
Court No. - 1
 

 
Case :- MISC. BENCH No. - 24734 of 2019
 

 
Petitioner :- M/S Kanchan Brick Field Thru. Prop. Girish Babu
 
Respondent :- State Of U.P. Thru. Prin. Secy. Pollution Deptt, Lko & Ors.
 
Counsel for Petitioner :- Surendra Pal,Vijayendra Prakash Tripat
 
Counsel for Respondent :- C.S.C.,Ashok Kumar Verma,Hemant Kumar Mishra
 

 
Hon'ble Pankaj Kumar Jaiswal,J.

Hon'ble Jaspreet Singh,J.

Heard Sri Vijayendra Prakash Tripathi, learned counsel for the petitioner, Sri Gopal Kumar Srivastava, learned Standing Counsel for respondent Nos.1, 3 and 4, Sri Hemant Kumar Mishra, learned Counsel for respondent No.5 and Sri Devesh Pathak, learned Counsel for respondent No.6.

The order impugned is appealable under Section 30 of The Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981 and therefore, learned counsel for the petitioner prays for withdrawal of the writ petition with liberty to file an appeal before the Appellate Authority.

Prayer made is allowed. With the aforesaid liberty, the petition is dismissed as withdrawn.
