Jharkhand High Court
Bhola Prasad vs The State Of Jharkhand on 12 October, 2018
            IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                          A.B.A. No.3950 of 2018

            Bhola Prasad                                  .....   Petitioner
                                       Versus
            The State of Jharkhand                        ....      Opposite Party

            CORAM:         HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE H.C. MISHRA

            For the Petitioner         :     Mr. Hemant Kr. Shikarwar, Advocate
            For the State              :     A. P.P.
                                       -----

4/12.10.2018 Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned counsel for the State.

Petitioner is apprehending his arrest in connection with Barkatha P.S. Case No.47 of 2013, corresponding to G.R. No.704 of 2013, for the offence under Section 379 of the Indian Penal Code, Section 33 of the Indian Forest Act and Sections 4 and 21 of the Pollution Control Act.

The prayer for anticipatory bail of the petitioner was earlier rejected by order dated 17.11.2014 in ABA No.1971 of 2014.

The petitioner has not surrendered in the Court below, absconding since then, and has renewed the prayer for anticipatory bail, which cannot be considered at this belated stage.

In the facts and circumstances of the case, this anticipatory bail application stands dismissed.

(H. C. Mishra, J.) R. Kumar
