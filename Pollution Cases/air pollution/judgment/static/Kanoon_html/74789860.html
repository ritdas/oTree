                           ORDER

D.H.WAGHELA, C.J. (Oral) :

The petitioner has sought quashing of the order dated 2.2.2013 of the Karnataka State Pollution Control Board and that order is made under the provisions of the Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981 and the Rules made therein.

2. The present petition is not required to be entertained in view of the observation made by the Apex Court in 3 paragraphs 40 and 41 of the Bhopal Gas Peedith Mahila Udyog Sangathan & Others v. Union of India & Others ((2012) 8 SCC

326).

3. Accordingly, the petition is dismissed with liberty to the petitioner to take such alternative remedy as they may be advised.
