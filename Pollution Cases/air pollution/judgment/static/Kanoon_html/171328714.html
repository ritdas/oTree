JUSTICE SOUMITRA SAIKIA 27.05.2020 (Ajai Lamba, C.J.) The Court proceedings have been conducted through Video-Conferencing in view of the present scenario so as to maintain social distancing.

2. It appears that the consent to operate stone crusher unit under the name and style of M/s T.G. Enterprise Stone Crusher Unit, New Socunoma village granted under Sections 25/26 of the Water (Prevention & control of Pollution) Act, 1974, as amended and under Section 21 of the Air (Prevention & Control of Pollution) Act, 1981 was in vogue till 2.8.2019. It has been contended on behalf of the respondents that the licence itself having expired, the issue raised by the petitioner is rendered academic. There is no cause of action as on date because in the absence of licence in any case the unit cannot be operated.

3. We hereby give time to learned counsel for the petitioner to take instructions in view of the changed circumstances.

4. List on 10.6.2020.

5. Let copy of this order be provided under the signature of the Court Master. 

                            JUDGE                           CHIEF JUSTICE
