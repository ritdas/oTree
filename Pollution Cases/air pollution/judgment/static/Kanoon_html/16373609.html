                                ORDER

Admit. Standing counsel takes notice on behalf of the respondents.

Heard both sides on the question of interim relief. The petitioner seeks a direction to the 2nd respondent and officers of the 1st respondent Board to consider applications for consent filed by the petitioner for operation of Diesel Generator sets as backup facility in telecommunication towers without insisting for payment of consent fee based on the total capital investment incurred for the installation of the entire telecommunication tower and amenities as demanded in Exts.P10, P11 and P12.

2. Learned counsel for the petitioner submits that consent fee has been prescribed in Appendix-II to Kerala Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Rules, 1994, which prescribes fee on the total capital investment of the industrial plant on a slab basis.
