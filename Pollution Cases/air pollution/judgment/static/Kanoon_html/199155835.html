Madhya Pradesh High Court
Ramnik Power And Alloys Pvt. Ltd. vs M.P. Pollution Control Board on 21 January, 2013
                            1 M.Cr.C No. 15250/2012



        21/1/2013
             Shri Saurabh Sunder, counsel for the petitioners.
             Shri Sameer Chile, Government Advocate for the
        respondent / State.

This petition has been preferred under Section 482 of Cr.P.C for seeking quashment of order dated 21.11.2012 (Annexure A/10) issued summon by respondent under Sections 25, 44 and 47 of Water (Prevention & Control of Pollution) Cess Act, 1977 along with Sections 21, 37, and 40 of The Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981.

Let notice of this petition be issued to respondents by RAD.

Steps within three working days. Let matter be listed on 18th February,2013 for consideration of stay application.

(Tarun Kumar Kaushal) JUDGE dcs/-
