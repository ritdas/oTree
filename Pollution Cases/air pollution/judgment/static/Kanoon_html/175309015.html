Jharkhand High Court
Jakir Mian vs The State Of Jharkhand on 19 August, 2015
                    IN THE HIGH COURT OF JHARKHAND AT RANCHI
                                B.A. No.6529 of 2015

             Jakir Mian                                      .....   Petitioner
                                        Versus
             The State of Jharkhand                          ....      Opposite Party

             CORAM:        HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE H.C. MISHRA

             For the Petitioner         :       Mr. Rashmi Kumari
             For the State              :       A. P.P.
                                        -----

3/19.08.2015

Heard learned counsel for the petitioner and learned counsel for the State.

Petitioner has been made accused for the offence under Section 379 of the Indian Penal Code, u/s 33 of the I.F. Act and u/ss 4 and 21 of the Pollution Control Act, in connection with Barkatha P.S. Case No.47 of 2013, corresponding to G.R. No.704 of 2013.

There is allegation against the petitioner of running a stone crusher without taking valid license and NOC.

In the facts and circumstances of the case, I am inclined to release the petitioner on bail. Accordingly, the petitioner Jakir Mian is directed to be released on bail, on furnishing bail bond of Rs.10,000/- (Rupees Ten Thousand) with two sureties of like amount each to the satisfaction of Smt. Garima Mishra, learned Judicial Magistrate, 1st Class, Hazaribagh, or her successor, in connection with Barkatha P.S. Case No.47 of 2013, corresponding to G.R. No.704 of 2013.

(H. C. Mishra, J) R.Kumar
