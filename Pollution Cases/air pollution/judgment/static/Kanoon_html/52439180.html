(PER : HONOURABLE THE ACTING CHIEF JUSTICE MR. VIJAY MANOHAR SAHAI) We have heard Mr.K.S. Nanavati, learned Senior Advocate assisted by Mr.Krunal Nanavati, learned counsel for Nanavati Associates for the petitioners.

An appeal under Section 33B of the Water (Prevention and Control and Pollution) Act, 1974 lies to the National Green Tribunal (Western Bench), Pune. This writ petition is ordered to be dismissed on the ground that the petitioners have an alternative remedy of appeal.
