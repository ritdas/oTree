                             JUDGMENT

The petitioner, aggrieved by orders passed by authority under the AIR (Prevention and Control of Pollution Act 1981) challenging orders of the original authority, has filed Appeal No: 10/2010 before the appellate authority. The petitioner now confines his relief for direction to the appellate authority to dispose of the same expeditiously.

2. Having heard the learned Government Pleader and Standing Counsel for the Kerala State Pollution Control Board, I dispose of this writ petition with a direction to the 2nd respondent to consider and pass orders on Appeal No:10/2010 as expeditiously as possible, at any rate, within one month from 12.04.2012, on which date, the appeal is stated to be posted for hearing.
