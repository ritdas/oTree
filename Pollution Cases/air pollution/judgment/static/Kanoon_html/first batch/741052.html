Rajasthan High Court
M/S Rao Jogendra Grit Udhyog vs Raj State Pollution Control on 12 August, 2009
    

 
 
 

 IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUDICATURE FOR RAJASTHAN AT
JAIPUR BENCH JAIPUR

ORDER

S.B.CIVIL WRIT PETITION NO.9685/2009
M/s.Rao Jogendra Grit Udhyog
Versus
Raj. State Pollution Control Board

  DATE OF ORDER   ---   August 12,2009

PRESENT 

HONBLE MR.JUSTICE PREM SHANKER ASOPA


Mr.S.K.Shukla,for the petitioner     

	Counsel inter alia submits that the Board alone is empowered u/s 31-A of Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981, while order impugned dated 21.7.2009 has been passed by the Member Secretary, to whom no power has been delegated by the authority competent under the Act, 1981. 
	Issue notice along with a copy of this order to respondents returnable within four weeks. Notices be given Dasti.

In the meanwhile, operation of the impugned order dated 21.7.2009 shall remain stayed till further orders.

(Prem Shanker Asopa)J.
