                        J U D G M E N T

Petitioner approached the Pollution Control Board with Ext.P4 application to obtain consent. The petitioner has been served with Ext.P8 notice, to show cause against violation of the provisions of the Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act,1981. In view of Ext.P4 application, there shall be a direction to the Pollution Control Board to consider the same within one month from the date of receipt of a copy of this judgment, after notice to the petitioner and other affected parties. Till a decision is taken by the Board, no coercive steps have been taken in the matter.

This writ petition is disposed of as above.
