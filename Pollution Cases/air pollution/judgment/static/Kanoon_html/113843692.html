     THIS WRIT PETITION COMING ON FOR ORDERS,
THIS DAY, THE COURT MADE THE FOLLOWING:

                                ORDER

Office has raised an objection regarding the maintainability of writ petition. As the matter is a Green Bench matter, it has to be listed before the Principal Bench only.

2. Learned counsel for petitioner states that petitioner may be permitted to withdraw the writ petition reserving liberty to avail of the alternative remedy under the Air (Prevention & Control of Pollution) Act, 1981.

3. In that view of the matter, writ petition is dismissed as not maintainable reserving liberty to the petitioner to avail of the statutory remedy, if so advised.

Office to return the certified copies of Annexures to petitioner's counsel forthwith, subject to filing of copies of same for the purpose of record.
