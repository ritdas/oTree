                                J U D G M E N T

The petitioner is seeking for a direction to issue consent to operate under Section 25 of the Water (Prevention & Control of Pollution) Act, 1974 and Section 21 of the Air (Prevention & Control of Pollution) Act, 1981 to the petitioner. The petitioner is a Private Limited Company engaged in the business of property development. A building permit has been sought for construction of a multi-storied apartment consisting of 136 apartments known as 'Kent Glass House'. True copy of the building permit has been produced as Ext.P1. It is stated that the construction has already been completed as per the approved plan. Ext.P2 is the copy of the occupancy certificate issued by the first respondent-Corporation. The building has been numbered and the property tax assessment is also over.

2. Heard the learned Standing Counsel for first and second respondents. It is submitted by the learned Standing Counsel for the second respondent that a decision will be taken which will be communicated to the petitioner within a period of three weeks. The same is recorded and the writ petition is, accordingly, disposed of. No costs.
