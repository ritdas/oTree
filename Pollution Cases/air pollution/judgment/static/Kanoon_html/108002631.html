Petitioner alleges violation of order dated 29.09.2011 passed by this court, operative part thereof reads as under:-

"4. Keeping in view the nature of the prayer made, we are of the considered view that it would be expedient to issue direction to respondent no. 2 to take cognizance of the legal notice sent by the petitioners and after due inspection of the area, respondent no. 2 shall take a reasoned decision in accordance with the provisions of Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974. If the respondent no. 5 is found to be violating the provisions of law/policy or rules then appropriate action be initiated against him. The needful be done within a period of two months from the date of receipt of a certified copy of this order. "

Learned counsel appearing for Pollution Control Board submits that necessary action has already been taken.
