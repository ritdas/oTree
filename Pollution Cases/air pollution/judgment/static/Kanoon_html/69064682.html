At this stage, Shri Sanjeev Kuthiala, learned counsel representing the applicants, states that the applicants shall satisfy the authorities in case authorities are directed to consider and decide their cases. Applicants are at liberty to approach this Court, if need so arises.

All the aforesaid applications are disposed of.
