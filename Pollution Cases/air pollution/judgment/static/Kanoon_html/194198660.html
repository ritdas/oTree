                             ORDER

The petitioner is arrayed as accused in C.C.No.47/2011 registered for offences punishable under Sections 25 and 26 and also under Sections 41(2), 43 and 44 of Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974. He has filed this petition to quash the proceedings interalia contenting that the complaint was not filed by the Authorised Officer as required under Section 49 of the Act.

2. I have heard Sri.S.G.Bhagawan, learned counsel for petitioner and Sri.Gururaj Joshi, learned counsel for respondent.

3. Perused the complaint and list of documents annexed to the complaint. In the list of documents, copy of authorization letter is mentioned at Sl.No.13.

-3-

4. If the petitioner (accused) feels that there was no proper authorization to initiate the complaint, he is at liberty to raise such a contention before the trial court.

5. The petition is dismissed with these observations.
