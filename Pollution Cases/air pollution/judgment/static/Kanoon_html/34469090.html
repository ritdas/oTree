Gurjeet Singh vs State Of Punjab And Ors on 20 April, 2018
            IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA
                         AT CHANDIGARH

                                          CWP No. 9516 of 2018
                                          Date of Decision : 20.04.2018

Gurjeet Singh                                          ...Petitioner

                                   Versus

State of Punjab and others                             ...Respondents

CORAM:        HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE AJAY KUMAR MITTAL
              HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ANUPINDER SINGH GREWAL

Present:   Ms. Shivali Banka, Advocate for the petitioner.
           ***

Ajay Kumar Mittal, J.(Oral)

1. The prayer in this petition filed under Articles 226/227 of the Constitution of India is for issuance of a writ in the nature of mandamus directing respondents No. 2 and 3 to take necessary steps to close the industry producing mineral water etc. at Village Ghataur, Tehsil Kharar under the name and style of 'M/s GB Enterprises', respondent No. 4 run by respondent No. 5 being in violation of provision of the water (Provision and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974 and Air (Provision and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981.

2. After arguing for sometime, learned counsel for the petitioner submitted that since certain factual matrix could not be incorporated in the writ petition, therefore, she may be allowed to withdraw the present writ petition with liberty to the petitioner to file fresh one on the same cause of action by furnishing better particulars.

3. Dismissed as withdrawn with liberty as prayed for.




                                             (AJAY KUMAR MITTAL)
