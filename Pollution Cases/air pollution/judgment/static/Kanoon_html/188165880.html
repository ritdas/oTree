                 ORAL ORDER

(Per: HONOURABLE MR. JUSTICE NAVIN SINHA) 5 24-04-2014 Counter affidavit has been filed on behalf of the Bihar State Pollution Control Board stating that 191 brick Patna High Court CWJC No.2643 of 2014 (5) dt.24-04-2014 2/2 kilns have been identified to which consent to establish has been given and only 12 of them have been given environmental clearance. Further survey is in progress. These units also require clearance under the Environment Protection Act. Show cause notice was issued to these 97 defaulting units as to why they be not directed to be closed down for violation of 'Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981. None of them responded to the show cause notice within the time specified and they have been served with the final closure order dated 21.4.2014 with a copy forwarded to the District Magistrate, Darbhanga for enforcing compliance by closure.

The District Magistrate, Darbhanga, shall file his affidavit with regard to the steps taken by him pursuant to directions given by the Bihar State Pollution Control Board.

By the next date, we expect the Pollution Control Board to place on record its affidavit with regard to the remaining brick kilns identified by it.

List on the 24th of June, 2014.

(Navin Sinha, J) (Prabhat KumarJha, J) K.C.jha/-
