M/S Rama Stone Crushing Udyod ... vs State Of Punjab And Ors on 13 August, 2015
                   IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA
                                    AT CHANDIGARH
                                            -.-
                                                  C.W.P. No. 15434 of 2015
                                                Date of Decision:-13.08.2015
           M/s Rama Stone Crushing Udyog Samiti   ..... Petitioner

           Versus

           State of Punjab & Ors.                                       .....Respondents

CORAM :- HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE SATISH KUMAR MITTAL.

HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE MAHAVIR S. CHAUHAN.

Present:- Mr. M.S.Virdi, Advocate, for the petitioner.

Mr. Gourav Garg Dhuriwala, DAG, Punjab.

Ms. Savita Tanwar, Advocate, for respondents No. 1, 3 and 4. SATISH KUMAR MITTAL, J.(Oral) In this writ petition, the petitioner is also seeking stay of implementation of notice dated 15.06.2015 (Annexure P/8), whereby Punjab Pollution Control Board, Amritsar has issued certain directions under Section 33-A of Water (Prevention & Control of Pollution) Act, 1974.

Written statement on behalf of respondents No. 1, 3 and 4 has been filed. The same is taken on record.

In view of the fact that against the impugned order dated 15.06.2015 (Annexure P/8), remedy of appeal has been provided under Section 33B (c) of the Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974, learned counsel prays that the petitioner may be permitted to withdraw this petition with liberty to avail the aforesaid remedy.

Dismissed as withdrawn with the aforesaid liberty.



                                                   ( SATISH KUMAR MITTAL)
                                                           JUDGE


                                                   (MAHAVIR S.CHAUHAN)
