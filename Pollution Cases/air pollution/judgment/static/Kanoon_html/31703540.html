M/S Hindustan Finishers vs State Of Punjab And Others on 18 November, 2013
                 IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA AT CHANDIGARH


                                            Civil Writ Petition No.25150 of 2013 (O&M)
                                            DATE OF DECISION: 18.11.2013

                M/s Hindustan Finishers
                                                                              .....Petitioner
                                                    versus

                State of Punjab and others
                                                                           .....Respondents



                CORAM:-        HON'BLE MR.JUSTICE SANJAY KISHAN KAUL, CHIEF JUSTICE
                               HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE AUGUSTINE GEORGE MASIH


                Present:       Mr.Rohit Sud, Advocate for the petitioner
                                    ..

SANJAY KISHAN KAUL, CHIEF JUSTICE: (Oral) Though the orders purport to be under Section 33-A of the Water (Prevention and Control Pollution Act) 1974 and Section 31-A of the Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution Act), 1981, which are appealable, the exercise by the competent authority is pleaded to be without jurisdiction and the authority acted in respect of the subject matter with which it was not concerned.

Even if the aforesaid be the position, in our view, nothing prevents the petitioner from approaching the National Green Tribunal against the impugned order. These pleas can easily be advanced there.

Learned counsel for the petitioner, faced with the aforesaid position, seeks to withdraw the petition with liberty to approach the National Green Tribunal.

Liberty granted.

Petition is dismissed as withdrawn.



                                                             ( SANJAY KISHAN KAUL )
                                                                  CHIEF JUSTICE


                18.11.2013                                (AUGUSTINE GEORGE MASIH)
