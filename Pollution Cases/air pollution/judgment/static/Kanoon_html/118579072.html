3/03.02.2015

I. A. No. 487 of 2015:

In this interlocutory application, a prayer has been made to allow the petitioner to challenge the order taking cognizance dated 09.01.2015 passed by Sri Surendra Sharma, learned Additional Chief Judicial Magistrate, Khunti.
It appears that during the pendency of the Cr.M.P. No. 3292 of 2013, cognizance was taken by the concerned court for the offences punishable under Section379/411/420 of the Indian Penal Code, Section 4(1), 54 of Jharkhand Minor Minerals Concession Rules, 2004 , Section 4(1), 21 MMRD Act, 1957 and Section 21 of the Air Pollution and Prohibition Act, 1981.
After going through the averments made in the interlocutory application, the same is allowed.
This application shall form part of the main application. I. A. No. 487 of 2015 is allowed and disposed of.

(Rongon Mukhopadhyay, J) R. Shekhar Cp 3
