M/S Soni Rice & General Mill vs State Of Punjab & Another on 31 May, 2012
             IN THE HIGH COURT OF PUNJAB AND HARYANA
                           AT CHANDIGARH

                          CWP No. 11189 of 2012
                      Date of decision : May 31, 2012


M/s Soni Rice & General Mill                                  .....Petitioner

                                     Vs.

State of Punjab & another                                     ....Respondents

                                    ******

CORAM: HON'BLE ACTING CHIEF JUSTICE HON'BLE MR. JUSTICE ALOK SINGH Present: Mr. Rajinder Singh Raj, Advocate, for the petitioner.

1. To be referred to the Reporters or not?

2. Whether the judgment should be reported in the Digest?

M.M. Kumar, Acg. C.J.

1. The order (Annexure P-1) is admittedly an appealable order as per the provisions of Section 31-B of Air (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1981 which provide for an appeal before National Green Tribunal.

2. Accordingly, the petitioner is relegated to the statutory remedy of appeal.

3. Petition stands disposed of.
